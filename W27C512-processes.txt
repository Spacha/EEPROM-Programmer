Winbond W27C512

ERASING (0 --> 1):
1. Set:
    nOE/Vpp: 14V
         A9: 14V
         A0: 0V
    All other address pins: 0V
    All data pins:          5V
2. Start:
    Pulse nCE: 0V


PROGRAMMING (1 --> 0):
1. Set:
    nOE/Vpp: 12V
    Address pins:   the desired address
    Input pins:     the desired input
2. Start:
    Pulse nCE: 0V
